Version 1.0.14
March 29, 2010

New Features:
- Selecting a sector will highlight it on the map.
- Selecting a battle or income location will select the sector in which the battle or mining occurred.

Bug Fixes:
- Crash introduced in 1.0.13 regarding ships without owners trying to check their owner's team name to filter it out of their names should be fixed.
- Derelict ships (those destroyed while still having hull or shield generators) no longer appear in the ship list.
- Sectors' borders no longer overlap on the map.
